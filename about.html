About ICT at UMP

This page provides an overview of the School of Computing & Mathematical Sciences, its mission, and its role in the university and community.

Sources: UMP official

School of Computing & Mathematical Sciences

The School of Computing and Mathematical Sciences at the University of Mpumalanga is dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement in the fields of information technology and mathematics.

Our programmes are designed to equip students with both theoretical foundations and hands-on experience, preparing them for careers in software development, data science, networking, and IT support.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality education and research that addresses regional and national development needs through ICT.

Vision: To be a leading hub for ICT innovation and digital transformation in Mpumalanga and beyond.

The school actively collaborates with industry and government to drive digital initiatives, including e-learning platforms, smart agriculture projects, and community IT literacy programmes.
