Volunteering & Projects

Students contribute to community projects such as teaching coding at local schools, developing apps for non-profits, and assisting with digital literacy campaigns.

  • Code4Change: Teach basic programming at Mbombela high schools.
  • HealthApp: Build a clinic appointment system with local healthcare providers.
  • AgriTech: IoT solutions for small-scale farmers (ongoing research).

Resources

  • Computer labs (Mbombela and Siyabuswa) with modern workstations.
  • Campus-wide Wi-Fi (eduroam).
  • Student Help Desk for technical support.
